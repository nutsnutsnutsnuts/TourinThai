Tham Phra Waterfall, Bueng Kan

Sentiment Analysis: Positive

Score: 0.89

Tham Phra Waterfall in Bueng Kan is a stunning three-tiered waterfall located in the Phu Wua Wildlife Sanctuary. Visitors can also explore the nearby Phu Tham Phra Monastery, making it a serene spot for both relaxation and adventure ​
